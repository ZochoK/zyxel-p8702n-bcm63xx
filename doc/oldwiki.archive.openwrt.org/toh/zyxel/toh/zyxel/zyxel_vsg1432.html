ZyXEL VSG1432

Under Construction!
This page is currently under construction. You can edit the article to help completing it.

Wireless router with 4-port switch, 802.11b/g/n

OpenWrt support

WIP. Not (officially) supported.

Supporting activities

Follow the discussion of possible support at this OpenWrt thread: http://forum.openwrt.org/viewtopic.php?id=62282

Patched Chaos Calmer version will boot and function as a wired router. Wireless doesn't work on boot but can be made to work by following instructions detailed in this post: http://superuser.com/questions/868231/wlan-on-openwrt-router

ADSL modem does not work.

Experimental firmware

A compiled firmware (Chaos Calmer) can be downloaded from https://drive.google.com/open?id=0B6MmeMg4IKvva09MYUIwaDdiTnc

Opening the case

Lift the back half of the rubber feet to reveal 4 screws. Remove the screws, then gently pry the very top of the case. Take it slowly, there is another antenna taped to the inside of the lid with some foil tape.

Serial

→ port.serial general information about the serial port, serial port cable, etc.

How to connect to the Serial Port of this specific device:

Serial header is labelled J3.

PIN1: 3.3V – don't connect!
PIN2: TX
PIN3: RX
PIN4: NC
PIN5: GND
